Welcome to AI Review Detector!

This website employs advanced technology to discern whether a given review was crafted by a human or generated by ChatGPT, an AI language model.
Powered by a sophisticated RoBERTa-based model, this predictive tool has been meticulously trained on a diverse dataset encompassing over 8000 reviews. Feel free to test the authenticity of your reviews!
